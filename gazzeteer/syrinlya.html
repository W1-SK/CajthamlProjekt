Syrinlya

Datum vis co

Syrinlya is a remote Uthodurnian outpost of clustered yurts on the southeastern coast of Foren in the Eiselcross archipelago, founded and overseen by Professor Gulrim Shalebrow for launching relic-hunting expeditions into the frozen wastes

Location

Perched atop a windswept plateau overlooking jagged ice floes, Syrinlya sits at the edge of the Frigid Depths, offering the last bastion of civilization before the inhospitable expanses of northern Wildemount

History

Originally established as a simple fishing hamlet, Syrinlya was formalized as an academic outpost circa 820 PD when Shalebrow and his scholarly cohort uncovered their first Aeorian relics in nearby ruins

Society

Home to roughly 182 residents—predominantly dwarves (72%) and elves (24%)—the community abides by an informal code of mutual support enforced by notable figures such as ‘The Buyer,’ a nonbinary elf managing the Buyer's Hut, and Morgo Delwur, a werebear whose vigilante justice keeps order.

Points of Interest

The Buyer’s Hut: The largest yurt where artifacts are cataloged and magically shipped back to Uthodurn via a stone chest portal

Shalebrow’s Excavation Camp: A series of tents and digging platforms bristling with arcane wards and glyphs, marking the site of ongoing Aeorian research.

Adventure Hooks

• A Dwendalian Crown agent seeks proof that the Assembly plans to exploit newly unearthed relics—escort them safely through Syrinlya’s defenses.
• A desperate merchant offers rare supplies in exchange for relic samples from the ruined Aeorian vaults just north of the outpost.
• Rumors tell of a hidden cache of timeworn journals buried beneath the ice, chronicling first contact with the Aeorian civilization.

DM Notes

Mechanically, treat Syrinlya as a frontier outpost (CR 2) with static defenses (wooden palisades AC 12, HP 100). The Buyer’s portal chest functions as a Leomund’s Secret Chest (save DC 16). Random expedition encounters can include remorhaz, winter wolves, or rival relic hunters. Players may earn Shalebrow’s favor (granting advantage on Arcana checks) by safely retrieving artifacts without damaging them.
