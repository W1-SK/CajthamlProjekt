Allowak’s Sanctuary

03.06.2025

Nestled one mile west of the great crater lies a barren, rocky hill whose surface never holds snow or ice. On its northern face, a yawning cavern mouth beckons curious travelers into the depths.

Cavern Description

Beyond the entrance stretches a domed chamber some sixty feet across. The walls are smooth and echo every footstep, and the air within is unnaturally still.

The Obelisk

At the center stands a fifteen-foot-tall obelisk of dark, reflective metal—so polished it seems like obsidian made living. A constant, low hum of abjuration magic radiates from its surface.

Magical Effects

Anyone who completes a long rest within 30 feet of the obelisk is plagued by vivid, unsettling dreams of alien landscapes and cryptic visions. These dreams ignore alignment and language barriers alike, leaving even the most stoic adventurers shaken.

Adventuring Hooks

• A local sage hires the party to investigate the ruin and recover any arcane secrets locked within the obelisk’s aura.
• Miners report that their picks crackle with magical energy near the hill—perhaps the stone can be harnessed for powerful wards.
• Rumors swirl of a lost time-mage who first enchanted the obelisk; finding their hidden journal could unlock its true purpose.

DM Notes

The obelisk’s abjuration aura is a Ward save DC 16. If the PCs dispel or remove the obelisk, they risk unleashing a psychic backlash (6d6 psychic damage on a failed Wisdom save DC 16, or half on success). Its metal face can reflect spells of 3rd level or lower back at the caster (user’s choice), once per round.
